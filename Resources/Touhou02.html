Touhou 2 - Story of Eastern Wonderland

Touhou 2 is the first danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Amusement Makers in 1997. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies and dodge the bullets.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 2 are very simple.
  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to shoot talismans, your basic attack. you want to hold this down at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • At the end and middle of every stage, a boss will show up. you must dodge the projectiles and shoot the boss until they die.

    • Items

    Sometimes when killing enemies, items will appear.
  • Point brick. Someties a purple brick with a japanese character on it will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Power brick. Sometimes a red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will make your attack stronger.
  • Bomb brick. Sometimes a green brick with a green B will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra bomb.
  • 1up brick. Sometimes a purple brick with a white 1up will appear. picking it up wil grant you one extra life
    At the top of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. Each Yin-Yang Orb is a live. you also have a 0th live.
  2. the white orb is your number of bombs, you can have up to five at a time.
  3. This is your power level. it increases every time you grab a Power brick.
  4. this is your high score.
  5. This is your current score.
  6. This is your current difficulty, you can change it from the main menu.
