Touhou 9 - Phantasmagoria of Flower View

Touhou 9 is a danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Amusement Makers in 1998. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies, dodge the bullets, and last longer then the other player.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 9 are not so simple.

    Singleplayer Controls

  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to rapidly press this at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • Hold down Z to use a charged attack at the expense of your meter.

    • Multiplayer Controls

    For P1,
  • Move left, right, up, and down with F, H, B, and T. Move diagonally by pressing R, Y, V, and N.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to rapidly press this at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • Hold down Z to use a charged attack at the expense of your meter.
    • For P2,
  • Move left, right, up, and down with 4, 6, 8, and 2. Move diagonally by pressing 7, 9, 1, and 3.
  • Press the left key to use your basic attack. you want to rapidly press this at all times.
  • Press the right key to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • Hold down the left key to use a charged attack at the expense of your meter.
    Across your entire screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. This is P1.
  2. These are your points, beneath your points is how many rounds you've won, you need two wins in order to win the game.
  3. The Yin-Yang Orbs are your HP, if they runs out you die.
  4. This is your combo.
  5. This is your attack gauge, which when used, will send an attack over to your opponent's field, depending on how much gauge is used.
  6. This number shows how many times a gauge attack has been used
  7. This number shows how many times a tier 4 gauge attack has been used
  8. This is your lives (Singleplayer Only)
  9. This is an idiot. (P2 in multiplayer)
