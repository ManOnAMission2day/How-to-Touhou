Touhou 8 - Imperishable Night

Touhou 8 is a danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Team Shanhai Alice in 2004 and was planned to be the last one in the series. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies and dodge the bullets.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 8 are rather simple.
  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to hold this down at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, as you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • At the end and middle of every stage, a boss will show up. you must dodge projectiles and shoot the boss until they die.
  • Press and hold SHIFT to slow down your movement. using this will also swap you to the second character chosen

    • Items

    Sometimes when killing enemies, items will appear.
  • Point brick. Someties a blue brick with a japanese character on it will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Large red brick. Sometimes a large red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will increase your power meter by 8.
  • Power brick. Sometimes a red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will make your attack stronger.
  • Bomb brick. Sometimes a green brick with a green B will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra bomb.
  • 1up brick. Sometimes a purple brick with a white 1up will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra life
  • Time points. whenever you kill an enemy, tiny see-through grey circles will appear, grabbing these will grant you extra points.
    On the right of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details

  4. This is your graze points, each time you graze a bullet, this value goes up.
  5. This is a total counter of every point brick you grab. reaching the stated cap will grant you a live.
