Touhou 5 - Mystic Square

Touhou 5 is a danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Amusement Makers in 1998. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies and dodge the bullets.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 5 are rather simple.
  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to hold this down at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, as you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • At the end and middle of every stage, a boss will show up. you must dodge projectiles and shoot the boss until they die.
  • Press and hold SHIFT to slow down your movement.

    • Items

    Sometimes when killing enemies, items will appear.
  • Point brick. Someties a purple brick with a japanese character on it will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Power brick. Sometimes a red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will make your attack stronger.
  • Bomb brick. Sometimes a green brick with a green B will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra bomb.
  • 1up brick. Sometimes a purple brick with a white 1up will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra life
  • Dream Block. somtimes a blue brick with a japanese character on it will appear, picking it up will increase the Dream meter.
    On the right of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. Each Yin-Yang Orb is a live. you also have a 0th live.
  2. the white orb is your number of bombs.
  3. This is your power level. it increases every time you grab a Power brick.
  4. this is your high score.
  5. This is your current score.
  6. This is your current difficulty, you can change it from the main menu.
  7. This is a total counter of every point brick you grab.
  8. This is a counter of the extra value of point bricks (increases every time you pick up a Point increase brick.)
  9. This is your graze points, each time you graze a bullet, this value goes up.
  10. This is the Dream Meter, exerytime you pick up Dream Blocks, other blocks, or just by surviving this value will increase. granting you extra points for everything you do (I think.)
