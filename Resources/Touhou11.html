Touhou 11 - Subterranean Animism

Touhou 11 is a danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Team Shanhai Alice in 2008. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies and dodge the bullets.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 11 are rather simple.
  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to hold this down at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility. be carful though, as using a bomb takes 1.00 power.
  • At the end and middle of every stage, a boss will show up. you must dodge projectiles and shoot the boss until they die.
  • Press and hold SHIFT to slow down your movement.
  • Some characters have special abilitys for specific shot-type, for instance, Reimu C can move at insane speeds when not slowing or attacking, or Reimu A can cross from one side to the other by double tapping against a wall when not attacking or slowing. i don't know what the rest of them are, that's up to you to figure out!

    • Items

    Sometimes when killing enemies, items will appear.
  • Point brick. Someties a blue brick with a japanese character on it will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Power brick. Sometimes a red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will make your attack stronger by 0.05.
  • Bomb brick. Sometimes a green star will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra bomb.
  • 1up fragment. Sometimes a purple star will appear. picking it up will grant you one piece of a life. collect 5 for a full life!
  • 1up brick. Sometimes a purple brick with a white 1up will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra life
  • Large red brick. Sometimes a large red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will increase your power meter by 1.00.
    On the right of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. Each Star is a live. you also have a 0th live.
  2. This is your power level. it increases every time you grab a Power brick. (Max. 4.00)
  3. This is the frame rate, it should be as close to 60.0 as possible.
  4. This is your high score.
  5. This is your current score.
  6. This is the value of your point blocks. the number shown is how much each point block is worth.
  7. This is the item line. crossing this with your character pulls all items on screen to you.
  8. This is your selected difficulty
  9. This is your graze points, each time you graze a bullet, this value goes up.
