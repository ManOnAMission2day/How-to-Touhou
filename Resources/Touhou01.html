Touhou 1 - Highly Responsive to Prayers

Touhou 1 is a breakout-style game created by ZUN and released by Amusement Makers in 1997. in the game, your task is simply to hit your bouncing Yin-Yang orb into the tiles to flip them over.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 1 are very simple.
  • Move left and right with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to throw a Talisman, a ranged projectile for hitting the Yin-Yang Orb or for hitting white dot projectiles.
  • Press X to swing your Gohei, a melee weapon for hitting the Yin-Yang Orb when it is nearby you. or for hitting white dot projectiles when they are nearby you.
  • By pressing X while moving, you can preform a slide attack in the direction you were moving.
  • Avoid getting hit by the Yin-Yang Orb. touching it will kill you.
  • Every 5 stages, a boss will show up. your talismans and gohei will do nothing to the boss, you must hit your Yin-Yang Orb into the boss in order to deal damage.

    • Combinations

    Touhou 1 features combination attacks to preform special moves. However, due to poor coding, these combination moves can be very inconsistent.
  • Bomb. A bomb is a powerful attack which will flip lots of tiles at once, or if you are on your last live, deal 2 points of damage to bosses. in order to bomb, i've found the most consistent method of bombing is both arrow keys + Z. be carful though, you have a limited supply of bombs.
  • Barage of Talismans. after sliding, sometimes you can press Z to release a barage of talismans.
  • Spin Kick. After preforming a slide, you can press the movement key the opposite direction you were moving, and sometimes you will preform spin kicks. you can preform up to three of these at a time.

    • Items

    Sometimes when flipping over tiles, items will appear.
  • Point orb. A grey orb with a large red P on it will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Bomb Extend block. sometimes a red brick with the words "Bomb Extend" will appear. picking it up will grant you one extra bomb.
    At the top of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. Each Reimu head is a live. You can get lives by getting enough points. You also have a 0th live.
  2. the white orb is your number of bombs, you can have up to five at a time.
  3. this is where the bosses HP would be listed.
  4. this is your high score.
  5. This is your current score.
  6. This is your highest combo.
  7. This is your current combo, you gain one point in combo each time your Yin-Yang Orb flips a tile without touching the ground.
  8. This is your timer, if the timer runs out, the game will start shooting extra projectiles at you until you die or compleate the stage.
  9. This is the stage you are on. there are 20 stages in the game.
  10. This is the difficulty you are playing on, it can be changed in the main menu settings.
