Touhou 15 - Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom

Touhou 15 is a danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Team Shanghai Alice in 2015. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies and dodge the bullets.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 15 are rather simple.
  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to hold this down at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility.
  • At the end and middle of every stage, a boss will show up. you must dodge projectiles and shoot the boss until they die.
  • Press and hold SHIFT to slow down your movement.

    • Items

    Sometimes when killing enemies, items will appear.
  • Point brick. Someties a blue brick with a japanese character on it will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Power brick. Sometimes a red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will make your attack stronger by 0.01.
  • Bomb star. Sometimes a green star will appear. picking it up will grant you one piece of a life. collect 8 for a full bomb!
  • 1up fragment. Sometimes a purple star will appear. picking it up will grant you one piece of a life. collect 3 for a full life!
  • Large red brick. Sometimes a large red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will increase your power meter by 0.25.
    On the right of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. Each heart is a live. you also have a 0th live.
  2. This is the frame rate, the average frame rate is 60.0.
  3. This is your high score.
  4. This is your current score.
  5. This is the value of your point blocks. the number shown is how much each point block is worth.
  6. This is the item line. crossing this with your character pulls all items on screen to you.
  7. This is your graze points, each time you graze a bullet, this value goes up. AND grazing bullets slows down all items on the screen.
  8. This is your power level. it increases every time you grab a Power brick. (Max. 4.00)
  9. These are your spellcards/bombs, use one to clear the screen and grant temporary invincibility.
