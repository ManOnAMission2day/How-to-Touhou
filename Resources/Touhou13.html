Touhou 13 - Ten Desires

Touhou 13 is a danmaku (Bullet Hell) shooting game created by ZUN and released by Team Shanghai Alice in 2011. in the game, your task is to shoot the enemies and dodge the bullets.

Controls

The controls for Touhou 13 are rather simple.
  • Move left, right, up, and down with the arrow keys.
  • Press Z to use your basic attack. you want to hold this down at all times.
  • Press X to bomb. this clears all the projectiles on screen and grants temporary invincibility.
  • At the end and middle of every stage, a boss will show up. you must dodge projectiles and shoot the boss until they die.
  • Press and hold SHIFT to slow down your movement.

    • Items

    Sometimes when killing enemies, items will appear. in this game, most bricks have been turned into blobs, the blobs do not fall down the screen, and instead sit at the top and fade out if you don't grab them fast enough.
  • Point blob. Someties a blue blob will appear, picking it up will grant you points.
  • Power brick. Sometimes a red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will make your attack stronger by 0.05.
  • Bomb blob. Sometimes a green blob will appear. picking it up will grant you one piece of a life. collect 8 for a full bomb!
  • 1up blob. Sometimes a purple blob will appear. picking it up will grant you one piece of a life. collect 8+ for a full life!
  • Large red brick. Sometimes a large red brick with a white P on it will appear, picking it up will increase your power meter by 0.25.
  • Grey blob. Grey blobs will appear, picking them up will increase you death meter (which is a good thing).
    On the right of your screen is your HUD, your HUD lists the following details
  1. Each heart is a live. you also have a 0th live.
  2. This is the frame rate, it should be as close to 60.0 as possible.
  3. This is your high score.
  4. This is your current score.
  5. This is the value of your point blobs. the number shown is how much each point block is worth.
  6. This is the item line. crossing this with your character pulls all items on screen to you.
  7. This is your selected difficulty
  8. This is your graze points, each time you graze a bullet, this value goes up.
  9. This is your power level. it increases every time you grab a Power brick. (Max. 4.00)
  10. This is the death meter, which fills up when you grab the grey blobs, when you die, depending on how many grey blobs you grabbed, you will get extra time to continue attacking before dying. the meter resets upon death.
  11. These are your spellcards/bombs, use one to clear the screen and grant temporary invincibility.
